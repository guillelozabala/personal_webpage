Hi! I'm Guillermo Martínez, a PhD candidate at CEMFI. My research interests lie in the economics of health, family and education.

Some of the questions my research aims to answer are: How can we improve health outcomes in illicit drug markets? How do parenting abilities influence household well-being? Are academic outcomes affected by environmental regulations?

I'm also a recipient of the Ramón Areces Foundation grant.
